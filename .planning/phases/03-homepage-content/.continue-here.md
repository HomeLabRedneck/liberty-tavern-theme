# BLOCKING CONSTRAINTS ‚Äî Read Before Anything Else

> These are not suggestions. Each constraint below was discovered through failure.
> Acknowledge each one explicitly before proceeding.

- [ ] CONSTRAINT: Real `.category-box-inner` direct children are `.category-box-heading` and `.category-box-stat` ‚Äî NOT badge/h3/description individually. Three rounds of CSS targeting the wrong elements proved this. Any grid-column rules on `.badge-wrapper`, `h3.category-title-link`, or `.category-description` as direct grid children of `.category-box-inner` have NO effect.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: `.category-details` and `.category-stat-info` classes do NOT exist in this Discourse install's categories_boxes DOM. Do not target them.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: 2-class specificity on `.category-boxes .category-box-inner` (display:grid) was overridden by Discourse's 3-class rule. Always use 3-class minimum: `.category-boxes .category-box .category-box-inner`.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: `width: auto !important` on `.category-boxes` ‚Äî Discourse sets `width: 100%`, so `margin-right: 64px` alone causes right overflow.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: `.wrap` must be fully zeroed (all 4 sides) on homepage routes.

**Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

## Critical Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Description | Severity | Prevention Mechanism |
|---------|-------------|----------|---------------------|
| Targeting assumed direct grid children | `.badge-wrapper`, `h3`, `.category-description` are NOT direct children of `.category-box-inner` ‚Äî they're inside `.category-box-heading` | blocking | Only target `.category-box-heading` and `.category-box-stat` as direct children of `.category-box-inner` |
| Nonexistent class selectors | `.category-details` and `.category-stat-info` don't exist; rules targeting them are silently ignored | blocking | Verify selector existence with live DOM before assuming |
| Low specificity on display override | 2-class selector for `display:grid !important` lost to Discourse's 3-class rule | advisory | Always match or exceed 3-class specificity when overriding Discourse layout |
